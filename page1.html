About Us

The Nazirite International Christian school started 11 years ago ( 2014) as a home school with my four chidren. We have never looked back

This vision was birthed 26 years ago ( 1998) when the Lord put it on my heart to raise him an " end time army") using Christian Education and parenting mentorship as the wheel.

And that is still our deepest conviction along with academic excellece and programmes that expand knowledge and skill learners to beat the present competition for university entry exams and the high competition in todays job market.



The name " Nazirite" defines the call upon this school. Children that are called and set apart for Gods kingdom purposes

The school finds its anchor in God and the authority of Gods word, the bible.

As we persue academic excellence and help learners find their God given calling, a comitment to consecration and holiness becomes central in the lives of these students

The 21st century is in need of graduates that will be academically empowered and spiritually grounded to navigate the challenging times

We seek to provide an excellent Education and Godly environment where learners are taught and mentored to walk in their God given callings and professions to enable them become authentic and honest at their jobs and God given work to impact the next generation


Join us at The NICS as we walk the journey of consecration and academic excellence for Gods glory

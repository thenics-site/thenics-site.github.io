About The ACE Curriculum

The ACE curriculum is well documented and planned in terms of equipping and raising a Godly individual. The curriculum incorporates biblical principles into known everyday theories thus anchoring all learning in God’s word and making it possible for the learners to live in God’s will daily. Furthermore, it is designed in such a way that the mode of instruction takes into account the fact that we are all created differently.

Uniqueness Of The ACE Curriculum

Each individual is unique and therefore students learn at different paces.

Each child is allowed to progress at the pace which he/she is naturally designed to understand the knowledge being passed on.

Proper understanding of what is being taught by individual learners, according to their natural ability.

Parental involvement in developing the natural abilities of the individual, ultimately raising a wholesome person.

Each child is enabled to operate within the calling that God has placed on their lives, with the full support and involvement of the parents/guardians.

The knowledge being passed on is aimed at facilitating the growth and development of the talent and ability that God gave to each individual.

The NICS Programmes

The NICS school offers programmes that are designed to provide a diverse and globally-oriented education, preparing students for a world that values intercultural understanding, a variety of skills, and knowledge in different areas of study, including hands-on practical lessons. The programmes focus on developing well-rounded individuals who can thrive in diverse environments, possess strong academic skills, and are knowledgeable about different gifts and talents. They Include:

Academic Excellence

We provide academic excellence through a rigorous and expanded curriculum that goes beyond traditional subjects. Our aim is to equip learners with practical skills relevant to the 21st-century job market and university requirements.

Global Skills

We aim to ensure that students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and collaboration skills—all essential for success in a globalized world.

Preperation for Higher Education

Our programmes provide a strong foundation for university studies, both locally and internationally.

Personal & Social Development: Holistic Education

Beyond academics, our diverse extracurricular activities promote creativity, artistic expression, historical knowledge, health consciousness, leadership development, and athletic growth.

Leadership & Service

Through these programmes, students are encouraged to take initiative, engage in community service, and develop leadership skills that prepare them to make a positive impact.
